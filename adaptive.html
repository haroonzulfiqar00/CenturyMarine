About Arizona Adaptive Water Sports

Founded and directed by Jo Crawford, AAWS provides a safe, welcoming environment where individuals of all abilities can discover the joy of being on the water. Their mission is simple and powerful: to ensure everyone has the opportunity to access the water and embrace the freedom and adventure it offers.

Events

With incredible events happening throughout the year, there are countless opportunities for participants and volunteers to get involved. Every gathering brings community, encouragement, and unforgettable memories on the water.

AAWS offers a wide range of exciting adaptive watersports and recreation. Kayaking, Waterskiing, Wake surfing, Wakeboarding, Boating, Tubing, Fishing, and more.
