About Century Marine

Our History

Century Marine was founded in 1979 by Bruce and Janet Bosley, pioneers who helped shape the wakeboarding, skiing, and boating scene in Arizona. Bruce continues to run the business and remains onsite daily, ensuring the same family-driven passion that started it all.

What We Offer

For decades, Century Marine has helped families enjoy the water with leading brands such as Centurion Boats, Supreme Boats, Sea Ray, Regal Boats, and Harris FloteBote, along with a large selection of preowned boats.

Awards & Recognitions

Century Marine has earned numerous industry awards and recognitions over the years, reflecting our commitment to exceptional customer service, trusted partnerships with leading manufacturers, and a long-standing reputation for excellence in boating.
